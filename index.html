POTASSIUM


Q: Is it a vitamin or a mineral? If it is a vitamin is it fat soluble or water soluble?



Potassium is a mineral.


Q: What are the 3 main functions of the vitamin/mineral?



Serves as an electrolyte, it regulates fluid balance, and is good for your nervous system!


Q: What are the daily recommendations for children, women, men, elderly?



Children: 2,500 mg, Adult men: 3,400 mg, Adult women: 2,600 mg, Elderly: 4,700 mg.


Q: What are the deficiency symptoms?



Under eating, eating disorders, etc.


Q: What are the toxicity symptoms?



Muscle Weakness, Numbness & Tingling etc.
